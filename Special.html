A Student's Prayer

Jesus, my God: I’m a loafer! I can’t deceive you as I do my parents. I’m facing my books but my mind wanders all over the place. They tell me I have no “method”. At other times they say that I am ungrateful, because I don’t take advantage of the opportunities which will never come my way again. My father does not get tired of telling me repeatedly that he had to work and study at the same time, and that in his days there was more “responsibility”. But even when I know he is right (which makes it worse for me), I go back to class and fool around with my classmates, ignore my teachers and at times even enjoy the luxury of being ironical, coming to class unprepared, studying between periods or on the way to the school (besides cheating in all almost all my written tests..) My God: I know, from the gospels that you were a real student ready to learn everything that was good and true. Change me and my foolish head so full of day dreams, so that I may become a good student. Please, make this come true... Amen.

A Teacher's Prayer

I want to teach my students how to live this life on earth To face its struggles and its strife And improve their worth Not just a lesson in a book Or how the rivers flow But how to choose the proper path wherever they may go To understand eternal truth And know the right from wrong And gather all the beauty of a flower and a song For I help the world to grow in wisdom and in grace Then I shall feel that I have won and I have filled my place And so I asked your guidance, God That I may do my part For character and confidence And happiness of heart. Amen.

Prayer for the Sick

We know, Lord God, that You have a special love for the sick. They are Your children and they are in pain. Give them the courage to discover a value in suffering. In your good time bring them true relief. Keep them content, happy and peaceful.
